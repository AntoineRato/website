Combining Left Turn! and Parking Jam 3D Mechanics

Posted on March 15, 2022

Hello, fellow game enthusiasts! Today, I want to share my experience in crafting a bonus puzzle level for one of the Voodoo games, Left Turn!

As a game developer, I love exploring new ways to enhance gameplay by combining elements from different games. In this case, I drew inspiration from the mechanics of Parking Jam 3D to create an engaging and challenging experience.

A Quick Overview of Left Turn!

Left Turn! is a game that focuses on simplicity and the addictive fun of tap-and-release mechanics.

Players control a car that moves forward when they press and stop when release the screen, with the ultimate goal of parking the cars one after the other while avoiding oncoming traffic.

The Bonus Puzzle Level: A Parking Jam Inspired Challenge

For the bonus puzzle level, I wanted to create an experience that would challenge players in a different way. To achieve this, I introduced elements from Parking Jam 3D, a game where players must navigate a crowded parking lot by moving cars forward or backward to create a clear path for their vehicles.

In this bonus level, players face a parking lot filled with cars that can only move in two directions. The objective is to strategically maneuver the cars to free up the correct ones, which in turn unlock others. The ultimate goal is to get all the cars out of the parking lot, adding a layer of strategy and depth to the core gameplay.

The Result: A Rewarding Blend of Two Games

By incorporating Parking Jam 3D mechanics into Left Turn!'s bonus level, I've managed to create a challenging and engaging experience that complements the base game. Players now have an additional layer of strategy to navigate, pushing their problem-solving skills to the limit and providing a more rewarding experience overall.

I believe it's essential to keep pushing the boundaries of what's possible within a game. By blending mechanics from different games, we can create unique and memorable experiences for our players.
