The Magic of Portal Mechanics: Breathing New Life into Hypercasual Mobile Games

Posted on February 2, 2022

Hello everyone! One of my favorite aspects of the job is experimenting with different mechanics and adapting them to fit the hypercasual mobile game space.

Today, I'd like to share with you my journey in integrating one of my favorite mechanics from Valve's iconic Portal series into few voodoo games, I’ll showcase 2 examples: “Shortcut Run” and “Cut This!”.

The Portal Twist in Shortcut Run

Shortcut Run is one of the most successful games I've had the pleasure of working on. For those who haven't played it, the game involves collecting boards to create paths that allow you to take shortcuts and race to the finish line. In my version, I decided to add a portal mechanic that players can utilize to gain an even more significant advantage during a race.

In this adaptation, players must collect enough boards to ensure they don't fall into the void upon exiting the portal. This addition adds an extra layer of strategy, as players must balance their resource usage with the potential benefits of taking the portal shortcut.

A Puzzling Portal Adventure

The second game I'd like to discuss is a unique puzzle game where players must cut objects to guide a ball towards the final point. In this game, I added an extra twist by incorporating portal mechanics as an alternative solution to each level.

By passing the ball through a portal, players can create new paths to complete the level, making it easier, more challenging, or even the only way to finish the level. This additional game mechanic adds depth and variety to the puzzle-solving experience and encourages players to think outside the box.

Conclusion

Integrating the portal mechanic from Valve's Portal series into my hypercasual mobile games has been an exciting and rewarding experience. Not only did it breathe new life into the games, but it also allowed me to explore different ways of using a beloved game mechanic in an entirely new context.

As a game developer, it's essential to keep innovating and experimenting with various game mechanics. By doing so, we can continue to create unique and engaging experiences for our players. So, stay tuned for more exciting projects and never be afraid to think outside the box!
